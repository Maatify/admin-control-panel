# Managing Admins

## Overview

Administrators are the authorized users who manage and operate the platform. Because they have access to sensitive information and controls, their accounts must be strictly managed and secured. This section of the control panel allows you to oversee who has access to the platform, what level of access they have, and how their accounts are secured.

## How to View Admins

To see a list of all administrators currently in the system:
1. Navigate to the **Admins** section from the main navigation menu.
2. Here, you will see a list of all existing administrator accounts.
3. You can click on any individual admin to view their full profile, which includes their assigned roles, active sessions, and notification history.

## Creating a New Admin

When you need to grant a new staff member access to the platform, you must create an account for them.
1. Go to the **Admins** section.
2. Click the **Create Admin** button.
3. Fill in the required details, such as their name and primary email address.
4. Save the new account. The new administrator will then need to complete the verification and setup process to access the panel.

## Editing Admin Profile

You can update the basic information of an existing administrator if it changes.
1. From the Admins list, click on the profile of the admin you wish to edit.
2. Select the **Edit Profile** option.
3. Update their name or notification preferences as needed.
4. Save your changes to apply them immediately.

## Managing Emails

An administrator can have multiple email addresses associated with their account.
1. Open the specific admin's profile and go to the **Emails** section.
2. To add a new email, enter the new address and submit it. The system will send a verification code to that new email to confirm they own it.
3. To replace a primary email or handle a failed verification, use the provided options next to the email address.
4. All new emails must be verified before they can be used for logging in or receiving important system alerts.

## Managing Sessions

A "session" represents an active login on a specific device or browser. To maintain security, you can view and manage where an admin is currently logged in.
1. Open the admin's profile and navigate to the **Sessions** tab.
2. You will see a list of all devices currently logged into this account.
3. If you see a device that is unrecognized or no longer in use, you can click **Revoke** to immediately log that device out.

## Permissions & Roles

To ensure that administrators only have access to what they need, the system uses Roles and Permissions.
1. In the admin's profile, navigate to the **Roles** or **Permissions** tab.
2. You can assign a pre-defined **Role** (like "Support Staff" or "Manager") which automatically grants a set of permissions.
3. If a role is no longer appropriate, you can unassign it.
4. You can also assign **Direct Permissions** for specific actions if an admin needs a unique level of access that doesn't fit a standard role.

## Important Notes

* **Security First:** Always ensure that new admins are given the minimum level of access necessary to perform their job.
* **Verification Required:** Changing an email address requires verification. An admin cannot use a new email address to log in until it has been successfully verified.
* **Account Tracking:** Every action taken by an administrator is recorded in the Activity Logs. This ensures accountability across the platform.
* **Immediate Revocation:** If an admin leaves the company or their account is compromised, you should immediately revoke all of their active sessions and remove their roles to secure the platform.
